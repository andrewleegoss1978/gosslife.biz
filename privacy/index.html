Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how gosslife.biz ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with personal productivity tools and applications operated by Andrew Goss.

1. Information We Access

Our applications (including AndrewMCP) may access email account data — such as messages, headers, and metadata — from services including Gmail and Yahoo Mail, solely on behalf of the account owner. This access is authenticated via industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocols and is used exclusively for personal productivity automation.

2. How We Use Information

Accessed data is used only to perform requested actions on behalf of the authorised account owner. We do not sell, share, rent, or otherwise disclose your data to any third parties.

3. Data Retention

We do not persistently store email content. Any transient in-memory processing occurs only during an active session and is not written to disk or transmitted externally.

4. Security

Access tokens are stored locally in environment files on the owner's personal devices and are never transmitted to third-party servers. All API communication is conducted over HTTPS.

5. Third-Party Services

Our tools connect to third-party APIs (Google, Yahoo). Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.
